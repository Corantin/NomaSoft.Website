# Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and only collect the information needed to respond to your
requests and improve our services. Data is stored securely and removed when no longer
required.

## Data we collect

- Contact details submitted via forms
- Project context you voluntarily share
- Technical telemetry strictly for analytics when enabled

## How we use data

We rely on the information to answer questions, prepare proposals, and deliver agreed
projects. We never sell personal data. Access is limited to NovaSoft engineers who need
it for work.

## Your rights

Contact us at [hello@novasoft.dev](mailto:hello@novasoft.dev) to request updates or
removal. We respond promptly to all requests.
